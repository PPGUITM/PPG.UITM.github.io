Location:
Kampung Sumangkap, Kudat, Sabah.

Address:Jalan Sumangkap, Kampung Pinawantai, 89050 Kudat, Sabah.
Getting There: To get to Sumangkap village, from Kota Kinabalu visitors can take a bus
headed in the direction of Kudat. Buses to Kudat can be taken from the Terminal
Bas Jalan Padang from 7.30am to 5pm daily.Bus fare would be approximately RM18 per
person, please communicate with the bus driver or bus conductor regarding the drop off point.
